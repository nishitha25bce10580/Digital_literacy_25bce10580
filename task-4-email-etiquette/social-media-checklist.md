# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts

## ✅ Do’s
- Maintain a professional and respectful tone in your posts
- Think before posting or sharing content
- Use privacy settings to protect your personal information
- Verify information before sharing it
- Build a positive and professional online presence

## ❌ Don’ts
- Do not share sensitive personal or financial information
- Avoid posting offensive or inappropriate content
- Do not engage in cyberbullying or online arguments
- Avoid clicking on suspicious links or messages
- Do not spread fake news or misinformation
