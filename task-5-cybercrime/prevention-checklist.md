# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Always check the sender's email address before clicking links
- Do not share OTPs, passwords, or personal details with anyone
- Use strong and unique passwords for different accounts
- Enable two-factor authentication wherever possible
- Avoid clicking on suspicious links or pop-ups

## 💰 UPI / Financial Safety
- Never approve unknown UPI payment requests
- Double-check UPI IDs before sending money

## 📱 General Safety
- Install apps only from trusted sources
- Keep your device and apps updated regularly

## 🚨 Reporting Cybercrime
- National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
- Helpline Number: 1930
